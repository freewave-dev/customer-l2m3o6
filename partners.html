T-Mobile Solution Partner — the relationship in detail.

Medical ANS works with T-Mobile's healthcare solution engineering team on joint deployments across hospitals, ambulatory networks, and specialty centers. We share design reviews, validation testing, and case studies. Where T-Mobile network services are in scope, our clients get carrier-side engineering on the design review — not just a sales contact.

Specifically, the partnership delivers:

  • Joint solution engineering for 5G fixed wireless and private cellular deployments
  • Access to T-Mobile's healthcare practice and case study library
  • Eligibility for T-Mobile-managed network services as part of an integrated managed offering
  • Co-deployed reference architectures that have shipped across active healthcare environments

Technology ecosystem

Vendor-neutral on hardware. Outcome-focused on the customer.

Routing & SD-WAN

Multiple SD-WAN platforms in production. We pick on application fit, security posture, and operational profile — not on what's in the channel.

Wireless

Security
