Industries we serve

Healthcare comes in many forms. So do its networks.

A community hospital and a multi-state outpatient network have almost nothing in common operationally — except the networks that hold them up. We've built for both, and the verticals between.

01 / Hospitals & health systems

Hospitals & health systems

Acute care brings the hardest constraints: OR connectivity, infusion pump fleets, imaging bandwidth, EHR latency, biomedical device sprawl, and an audit posture that gets reviewed every year.

We deliver carrier-grade WAN, zero-trust segmentation, IoMT enablement, and 24×7 managed operations under one team. Regional systems use us to consolidate carriers and modernize architecture without losing institutional knowledge.

02 / Ambulatory & outpatient

Ambulatory & outpatient networks

The economics of outpatient care depend on opening new sites fast and operating them lean. Most networks weren't built to do either.

We deploy SD-WAN with 5G fixed wireless as a real second leg, zero-touch site provisioning, and centralized policy so a 60-clinic network operates with the network overhead of a 6-clinic one.

03 / Specialty clinics & cancer centers

Specialty clinics & cancer centers

Specialty care depends on imaging, infusion, and remote-monitoring workloads that don't tolerate jitter or latency. The clinical floor is a high-density IoMT environment.

We deliver private 5G, IoMT enablement, and engineered QoS for imaging and clinical video. We've stood up private cellular in radiation oncology and infusion centers where Wi-Fi alone wasn't going to make it.

04 / Long-term care & post-acute

Long-term care & post-acute

Long-term care faces tightening regulatory pressure on connectivity, resident-facing services, and remote monitoring — alongside historically thin IT budgets.

We design and operate networks engineered for the LTC reality: lean staffing, multi-site footprint, resident-facing services, and resident-monitoring telemetry that has to be both private and reliable.

05 / Behavioral health

Behavioral health

Behavioral health is increasingly hybrid: telehealth-first intake, in-person care, remote-patient-monitoring for crisis prevention. Networks need to support video as a first-class clinical channel — at scale, with stable latency.

We engineer telehealth-grade QoS, secure remote-clinician access, and unified communications integration that holds up at peak clinic load.
