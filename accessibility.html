Accessibility Statement

Last updated: January 2026

Our commitment

Medical ANS is committed to making medicalans.com usable for the widest possible audience, including people who rely on assistive technology.

We design and build with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA as our standard. Where we fall short, we treat it as a defect.

What we do

Semantic HTML, descriptive page titles and landmarks, and a skip-to-content link on every page.

Text contrast ratios that meet or exceed WCAG AA. Focus states on all interactive elements. Keyboard navigation across the site.

Alt text on all meaningful images. Decorative graphics marked accordingly.

Forms with associated labels, error states announced to assistive technology, and a contact channel that doesn't depend on a single input method.

Known gaps

Where third-party content, downloadable assets, or embedded video appear, they may not yet meet our standard. We're working through these on a published schedule.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on medicalans.com, please write to accessibility@medicalans.com. We aim to respond within five business days, and we treat accessibility reports the same way we treat security reports — as a defect that needs fixing, not a complaint.
